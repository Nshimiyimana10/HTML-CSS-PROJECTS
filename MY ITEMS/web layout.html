Gorillas are most attractive in Rwanda
Beautiful Gorillas

In Rwanda,Gorillas are most lovable animals which represent the beauty of the country.

Most tourists come in Rwanda to visit the gorillas.
Every year, the country organize the ceremony called"KWITA IZINA".It is a ceremony which attracts many people allover the world where they are interested in this act of naming the new born of gorillas.
